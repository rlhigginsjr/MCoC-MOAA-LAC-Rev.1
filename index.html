Current Priority: Aligning Veteran Recognition for NOAA & USPHS Officers

Michigan law does not recognize commissioned officers of the U.S. Public Health Service (USPHS) and National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration (NOAA) as veterans — even though federal law (38 USC) does. These four House bills would correct that gap.
